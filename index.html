Sustenance

code.fun.do

Sustenance is a Universal Microsoft Application that was developed in C# as a part of the Microsoft Acad-Accelerator programme. It simulated a Food Web to highlight the role played by different species in Environment. Technically speaking, it required implementation of Data Structures for different species, potions and industries, integration with Windows Live accounts and algorithms for Artificial Intelligence

This is a game targeted for all platforms that creates a simulation of life on Earth which the user sees as an inter-connected web and tries to save creatures against deadly pathogens, gene mutations, poisons, pollution and the immense threats posed to wildlife nowadays.

Significance

Sustenance focuses on a critical aspect of Today’s World – Environment! Designed for all age groups, especially children, it not only sensitizes them towards environmental threats, but also helps understand Nature and Wildlife through food webs, information cards and different diseases. Sustenance is a Strategic game which portrays something as indispensable and vulnerable as the Environment in fun-loving way, while serving multiple purposes – Education, Awareness and Entertainment. It is the perfect tool to affect young generation positively.

Introduction – The Plot

The game is modeled on a Virtual World ‘Utopia’, where the soil, air, water, effective sunlight, different plants, animals, humans and even Industries are mutually dependent on each other for survival and prosperity. The quality and populations of these species changes continuously as functions of the Biotic and Abiotic components of the Environment. The different Species are the part of an elaborate Food-Web which also links to Humans, Industries, and the Abiotic Environment. They however, are caught in the crossfire between ‘Bob’ and ‘Marley’. The player, ‘Bob’ must fulfill his destiny, his Karma – To sustain and preserve life on earth using Heavenly Potions and Industrial Concoctions. On the other hand, ‘Marley’ (which shall be the AI) wants to convert Utopia into a cursed wasteland – using Hellish Poisons and Industrial Wastes.
